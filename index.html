Educational beacons

Android application that portrays the use of beacons in both secure and insecure settings.

Download Download View Educational beacons on GitHub

Introduction

In the ever increasing field of technology, new technology is bound to pop up. For the average joe, contextual technology is becoming more and more impact in their lives. Whether it's social media applications asking them to make a post in the event of a disaster so that their family knows they're ok to a simple "I'm at the coffee shop!" post.

Our application

We have developed a solution that enables any university to reliably relay information to their students through an application that once it detects a beacon, it will notify the user. The information will be a tap away.

Features

  • Eddystone-URL support
  • Eddystone-EID support
  • Configurable, just declare what URL the application should look after
